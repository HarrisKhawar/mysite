ABOUT ME

I am a bachelors student at Amherst College majoring in Computer Science and German.

Born in Pakistan, I spent the first 15 years of my life changing schools across Lahore. At the age of 16, I left Pakistan for the first time and began residing in Sri Lanka. From then on I have visited several countries including Dubai, Maldives, Qatar, Poland, France etc. (my travel logs can be found here). At 23 years of age there is not a lot I am, but a lot I aspire to be. From k-means clustering algorithm to Sumerian polytheism, almost anything can spark my interest.

Currently, I am studying at the University of Göttingen, Germany under a year-long study abroad program.
